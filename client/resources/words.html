Words of Wisdom

- Ron Swanson

Very Good Building and Development Co. was founded by Ron Swanson circa 2014.

Ordinarily, Ron Swanson would never allow any personal data relating to Ron Swanson or related persons to be released on the internet, where anyone could learn about him.

However, seeing as he left the company in 2017 and gave it to his brother, the marketing team decided that the company needed a website and not just word-of-mouth advertisement.

Luckily, Ron is hopelessly unable to work modern technology that does not relate to food and will therefore likely never know this website exists.
