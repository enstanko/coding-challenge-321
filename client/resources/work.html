Very Good Building and Development Company

Gryzzl Campus: Pawnee, Indiana

Very Good Building and Development Co. worked with Gryzzl to build their new headquarters in 2017. Originally, Gryzzl tried to buy a large piece of land to bulldoze for their campus, but due to the meddling of local ex-councilwoman Leslie Knope, Gryzzl instead bought land in the worst district in town in order to gentrify it.

Disclaimer: Ron Swanson does not endorse Gryzzl. In fact, Ron Swanson once shot down a Gryzzl drone via shotgun. Don't trust technology.

Morningstar Apartments: Pawnee, Indiana

Very Good Building and Development Co. built an apartment building during the mid-2010s. The land once consisted of a poor quality home and a pit of dirt in which multiple people suffered injuries. This piece of land caused many conflicts between local councilmen.
